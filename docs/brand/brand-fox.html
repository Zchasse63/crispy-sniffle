Meet Scout.

A separate universe, designed on its own terms: Scout is a fox — the animal that knows every trail, every shortcut, every good spot in the territory. The question isn't whether a fox; it's what register: a sharp geometric icon, a living character who talks to you, or a heritage scouting badge.
